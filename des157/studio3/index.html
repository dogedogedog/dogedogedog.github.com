Instruction:

Life is like a boxing game: you never know what is going to happen next.

Today we have two fighters. When you are ready, click the Fight button below the banner and see which amazing PIG fighter you will get today. Each turn the current fighter has a chance to attack, and the fighter will get points based on the hooks.

The first fighter to get 25 points or higher wins!!
